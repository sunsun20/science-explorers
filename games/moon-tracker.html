Bonus · Many Cultures, One Sky

Many cultures, one sky

The first thin crescent — hilal — marks the start of a new month in lunar calendars around the world. Here in the UAE it begins Ramadan and Eid; other moon calendars around the world watch for the very same crescent.

💡 Quick Tips

The Moon makes no light of its own — it reflects sunlight, like a ball lit by a flashlight.
Waxing 🌒→🌕 = the lit part is growing, lit on the right.
Waning 🌖→🌘 = the lit part is shrinking, lit on the left.
Try it at home: in a dark room, shine a flashlight (the Sun) on a ball (the Moon) and turn the ball around — you can make every phase yourself.
