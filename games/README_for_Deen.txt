SCIENCE EXPLORERS — GAMES
A little arcade of Grade-5 science games for your class.

--------------------------------------------------
HOW TO OPEN
--------------------------------------------------
Just double-click  index.html
It opens in any web browser (Chrome, Safari, Edge, Firefox).
That is the menu page — from there, tap a game to play.

You do NOT need the internet. Everything runs right on the
device, even with Wi-Fi switched off. It also works on a
tablet with touch, and on a laptop with a mouse.

--------------------------------------------------

--------------------------------------------------
GOOD TO KNOW
--------------------------------------------------
- Made for 10-year-olds, matched to Grade-5 science (NGSS).
- Everyday examples first (kitchen, playground, pets, weather,
  sport) so any child can join in. A local UAE touch appears
  as a friendly bonus, never the only way in.
- For everyone: gender-neutral wording ("students", "players")
  and characters any child can relate to.
- Culturally respectful: halal-friendly examples (no pork,
  no alcohol), modest characters, and the Moon shown as a
  "many cultures, one sky" connection that includes everyone.
- Key science words are shown in English AND Arabic.
- Friendly points and kind feedback — when an answer is wrong,
  the game gently explains why and lets the player try again.
- There are soft "blip" sounds. A speaker button in the top
  corner mutes/unmutes them anytime.

--------------------------------------------------
TIPS FOR THE CLASSROOM
--------------------------------------------------
- To share: copy this whole folder onto a USB stick or the
  tablet. Keep all the files together in the same folder.
- Each "Play Again / Restart" button lets students try as
  many times as they like.
- Nothing is saved to the internet and no sign-in is needed.

Have fun, and happy exploring!
