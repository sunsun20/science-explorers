🔺 The Energy Pyramid & the 10% Rule

When energy passes to the next level, only about 10% moves up. The other 90% is used for moving, growing and staying warm, and is lost as heat. Level 1 (producers) has the most energy, so it is the widest. That is why top predators are always rare.

A food chain shows one straight path. But in nature most animals eat more than one thing, so many chains overlap into a food web. One producer can feed several animals that branch out. A web is a better model because if one animal runs low, others have backup food.
