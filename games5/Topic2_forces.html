🔬 Standard 5-PS2-1 recap: Earth's gravitational force on objects at its surface is always directed downward — toward Earth's centre. It never switches off: a rising ball, a ball at the top of its arc, and a ball thrown sideways are all pulled straight down. With no air, heavy and light objects fall at the same rate. Evidence: released objects fall to the ground, rivers flow downhill, raindrops fall down.
